Pantograph Marine Wiper System

A dual-arm marine wiper solution developed for reliable visibility, wide wiping coverage and adaptable operation on different marine window geometries.

Overview

Designed for clear visibility

Pantograph wipers are developed to remove rain, seawater and contaminants from marine windows, helping maintain a safer field of vision during navigation.

Adaptable to window geometry

Motor position, park direction, arm length and wiping area can be configured according to the glass shape, operator field of view and project requirements.

Technical highlights

Mechanism

  • Dual-arm pantograph motion
  • Adjustable wiping angle
  • Top / bottom / side installation
  • Maximum sweep angle up to 100°

Motor & control

  • 24V DC motor
  • Self-parking function
  • Control panel integration

Arms & blades

  • Arm length selection
  • Blade length selection
  • Wide wiping coverage
  • Curved surface adaptability

Auxiliary systems

  • Integrated washer system
  • Spray nozzle option
  • Air / wash functions
  • Heater activation via control system
