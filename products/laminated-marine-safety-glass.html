Overview

Safety glass construction

Laminated glass combines two or more glass layers with an interlayer, helping the glazing remain bonded after breakage and improving onboard safety.

Performance-based selection

Glass thickness, interlayer type and coating selection can be adjusted according to solar, acoustic, thermal and project-specific requirements.

Technical range

Typical build-ups

  • 5 + 1.52 PVB + 5 mm
  • 6 + 1.52 PVB + 6 mm
  • Custom glass thicknesses
  • Multi-layer safety combinations

Interlayers

  • Clear PVB
  • Acoustic PVB option
  • SGP option
  • DG41 / SGP combinations

Performance options

  • UV reduction
  • Improved sound insulation
  • Solar control combinations
  • Required U-value calculations

Applications

  • Marine windows
  • Side scuttles
  • Wheelhouse glazing
  • Project-specific assemblies
