Overview

Insulated glass construction

Double glazed configurations combine multiple glass layers with an air or gas cavity, supporting improved thermal insulation and onboard comfort.

Project-specific performance

Glass thickness, cavity, coatings and heating options can be selected according to U-value, solar factor, daylight and condensation requirements.
