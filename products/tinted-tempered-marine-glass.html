What this sample shows

A compact demonstration of Karaca’s tinted tempered glass capability, including custom sizing, shape flexibility and compatibility with marine window and side scuttle applications.

Visual and functional value

Tinted tempered glass can support solar control, glare reduction and a refined exterior appearance depending on the project and selected tint.

Project-based processing

Glass can be produced according to project requirements including size, shape, edge finishing, holes, cut-outs and integration into wider glazing systems.
