Overview

Tinted tempered marine glass capability, including custom sizing, shape flexibility and compatibility with marine window and side scuttle applications.

Visual and functional value

Tinted tempered glass can support solar control, glare reduction and a refined exterior appearance depending on the project and selected tint.

Project-based processing

Glass can be produced according to project requirements including size, shape, edge finishing, holes, cut-outs and integration into wider glazing systems.

Available options

Final configuration depends on vessel type, glazing location and project requirements.

Glass build-up

  • Monolithic tempered glass
  • Laminated combinations
  • Double glazed combinations
  • Electrically heated combinations

Tint and appearance

  • Grey / smoke toned appearance
  • Project-specific tint selection
  • Visual consistency for product groups
  • Clear and tinted combinations

Processing

  • Custom dimensions
  • Shape cutting
  • Polished edges
  • Holes and cut-outs

Applications

  • Marine windows
  • Side scuttles
  • Doors and glazing units
  • Project-specific assemblies

Performance focus

Tinted and coated glass solutions can support solar control, glare reduction, privacy and exterior appearance depending on the selected glass build-up.

Solar and visual comfort

Reflective and tinted glass options can help reduce solar heat gain and control sunlight intensity while supporting a refined exterior appearance.

Marine glazing selection

Final selection should consider daylight transmittance, outdoor reflectance, solar factor, U-value and project-specific glass build-up.
